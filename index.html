The handicrafts and art industry in India, though rich in tradition, has historically faced challenges. It is labor-intensive and often unregulated, meaning artisans don't always get fair pay for their work. The middlemen who buy from artisans often take the majority of the profit, leaving the creators with very little. This lack of exposure puts the industry "on the verge of extinction". Platforms like Apkimart are directly combating this issue by connecting artisans with customers worldwide, ensuring they receive proper value for their skills and helping preserve these beautiful traditions.
